## The ten lessons

Each lesson is a short video (10 to 25 minutes) and a written companion with examples, templates, and the relevant pages from Mirella's cow-incident playbook. Do them in order the first time through. After that, jump around.

**Lesson 01 — The First Hour Decides The Rest**
_18 min · Video + Notes_
Why the opening sixty minutes of a reputation crisis matter more than the next six weeks. The three things to do immediately. The three things not to.

**Lesson 02 — Stop Explaining. Produce Evidence.**
_14 min · Video + Notes_
The single hardest habit to break, and the one that has saved more towers than any spell. Why explanation extends a panic and evidence ends one.

**Lesson 03 — Acknowledge The Fear, Not The Accusation**
_22 min · Video + Notes_
Two different things. Most wizards argue the accusation and leave the fear alone. Do the opposite. Here's how.

**Lesson 04 — The Three Frames**
_25 min · Video + Notes_
Mirella's core teaching. Every accusation gets shaped into one of three frames in public memory. Learn to spot which one is forming. Learn to reshape it before it sets.

**Lesson 05 — On Not Lying**
_19 min · Video + Notes_
Why spin based on falsehood always collapses, usually at the worst possible moment. What honest reframing looks like. Where the line is.

**Lesson 06 — The Ritual Of Reparation**
_16 min · Video + Notes_
Paying for the hammer that didn't get broken. Why symbolic gestures land harder than you'd expect, and how to choose them without looking like you're admitting fault.

**Lesson 07 — Changing The Song In The Tavern**
_21 min · Video + Notes_
Narrative displacement through bards, barn-raisings, and the right verses emphasized at the right time. Not spin. Surfacing the true story that wasn't being told.

**Lesson 08 — When The Accusation Is True**
_24 min · Video + Notes_
The hardest chapter. What to do when the villagers are, regrettably, correct. How to manage a crisis of your own making without losing your practice, your freedom, or your self-respect.

**Lesson 09 — Building Reputation Before You Need It**
_17 min · Video + Notes_
The healed gout, the fixed well, the blessed spring planting. Why the cheapest PR investment is the one you make in a year without crises.

**Lesson 10 — A Post-Mortem, Done Right**
_15 min · Video + Notes_
Once the crisis is over, the work isn't. How to extract the real lessons, update your playbook, and avoid the version of the story where you congratulate yourself and learn nothing.
