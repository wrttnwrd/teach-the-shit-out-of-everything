# Public Relations

Because the best wards in the world won't help you if the villagers are already coming up the hill.

> **TL;DR** The locals don't have to love you. They just have to stop lighting your tower on fire. We handle the difference with bards, barn-raisings, and a strategic absence of visible horrors.

Here is the single most underappreciated fact in the whole business: **the mob is always the last threat, and always the worst one.** Every wizard who has lost a tower has lost it to people holding farm implements.[^1] Not rival archmages. Not demons. Farmers. With torches. Furious about something that was, statistically, a misunderstanding.

We cannot make the farmers like you. We can make them not light things on fire. That gap is where we work.

## The cow incident, explained

Let me spend a minute on this, because it illustrates the whole practice.

A wizard we'll call "Archibald" was, last autumn, accused by the village of turning a local child into a cow. The wizard had done no such thing.[^2] The child was fine. The cow was a cow. The two had simply been in the same barn at roughly the same time, and the village had done its own arithmetic.

By the time we arrived, three things were true:

- The villagers were already gathering.
- Archibald's first instinct was to explain the transmutation schools in detail, which would have been a disaster.
- The actual child was home, eating supper, and would have been fine to simply _produce_.

We produced the child. We paid the miller for the inconvenience of the accusation. We had a bard in the tavern by nightfall with a new song about Archibald healing a sick goat the previous year. (He had. We'd looked it up.) Tower intact. Reputation recovered within a month.

Total cost: less than a week of Archibald's research time. Total cost of _not_ calling us: approximately one tower.

## What this service is not

- **It's not spin.** We don't manufacture stories. We surface the true ones that weren't being told.
- **It's not intimidation.** Making peasants afraid of you works for about six months and then they burn the tower down anyway.
- **It's not mind control.** This is in our contract in four places. We will not do this. Please stop asking.
