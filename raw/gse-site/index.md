## Why we exist

I've spent a lot of years in this business, and I'll tell you what I've learned:

**Wizards are paranoid, and they're right to be.** Everyone _is_ after your research. But paranoia without a plan is just anxiety with a spellbook.[^2]

**Wizards are not street smart.** I once watched a fifteenth-level evoker hand a "royal tax collector" six pounds of raw mithril because the man had a _very_ convincing seal. The seal was made of cheese. Good cheese, but cheese.

**Wizards have a PR problem.** One cow. One single, solitary cow. Allegedly a cow, even — we were never able to confirm the cow. And the tower burned anyway.

Three problems. Three services. We don't sell you things you don't need, and we don't promise things magic can't do.

## Who we are

We're named after the _slaad_, a creature of pure chaos, and the color green, which is what we wear when we're trying to blend in near your topiary. The joke is that order and chaos work together here. It's a better joke if you're already in the business.

Our bodyguards come from retired adventuring parties. Our infosec team is a coalition of ex-thieves who got tired of the thieves' guild politics. Our PR staff are bards who figured out that reputation is a longer con than a tavern brawl.
