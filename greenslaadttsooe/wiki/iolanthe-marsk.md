## Profile

- **Discipline**: Divination
- **Location**: Tower approximately one day north of Waterdeep
- **Household**: Lives alone. One housekeeper from the village (mornings only). No guards.
- **Defenses**: Standard abjuration wards on the perimeter, nothing custom.
- **Village relationship**: Positive — she brought a baby through a hard labor last winter. Two miles from the village.
- **Budget**: ~20 gold/month (below GSE's smallest retainer of 60 gold/month)
- **Referred by**: GSE's "Divination Is Not A Security Strategy" blog post
- **Rep**: [[jenna-rill]]

## Discovery call

Iolanthe called after reading GSE's divination post. Her initial reaction was fury, then reconsideration, then the call — "the reaction we hope for, roughly in that order," per Jenna.

She is motivated by the data rather than an active incident: "I'd like to not be the diviner in the poisoned-chef story." (This references the anecdote in the divination post about a diviner whose chef poisoned him because "attempt on my life" didn't match his mental model of the threat.)

### Outcome

- **Assessment booked**: One-time, 100 gold, two-day on-site tower walk. Written brief covering what to do, what to do herself, and what requires outside help. Many diviners in similar situations implement 90% themselves.
- **Waitlisted**: Added to the solo-practitioner tier waitlist (~14 people). The tier concept (remote monitoring, quarterly on-site, sending-stone access) has been discussed internally for six months with no product specs. See [[operational-gaps]].
- **Urgent-finding commitment**: If the assessment finds something urgent, GSE will help figure out next steps even if Iolanthe doesn't hire for remediation.

(source: call-04-prospect-physical-iolanthe.md)

Jenna flagged Iolanthe as a potential reference client if she eventually upgrades — she has a public following (social media presence noted as "diviner_iolanthe") that could be valuable. (source: call-04-prospect-physical-iolanthe.md)
