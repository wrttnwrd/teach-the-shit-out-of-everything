# Brief: The Three Frames

**Answers questions from questions.md:** Q16 (accusation is true) in part; Q26 (institutional PR methodology). Fills the single largest content gap on the GSE site — Mirella's core methodology, currently locked behind the Lesson 04 video.

**Size / type:** Teach-big · Upgrade (the methodology exists privately). Brand-defining if it lands. This is the piece that makes the rest of the site stop feeling like it's hiding the ball.

## Reader

Two audiences at once.

- **Primary:** any wizard in or adjacent to a reputation crisis who wants a real framework, not firefighting.
- **Secondary:** communications-professional peers who will cite it — which, in TTSOOE terms, is how teach-big pieces earn their links and dwell time.

## Concrete problem manifestations

- You've been accused and you can feel the story taking a shape you don't control.
- You're deciding whether to respond, when, and how, and the decisions feel arbitrary.
- Someone tells you "the narrative has set" and you don't know what that means operationally.

## Brand-agnostic teaching (biggest part)

> **This brief explicitly requires Mirella's authorization and involvement before writing — it's her IP.** Do not draft without her.

If authorized, the teaching should include:

- **What a "frame" is operationally** — not a narrative, not spin, but the mental shape the audience uses to sort subsequent information. Ground in [[narrative-timing]].
- **The three frames, named and explained by Mirella.** Content lives in Lesson 04 and the internal curriculum. Do not infer them from the public material — get them from her.
- **How to identify which frame is forming** — what villagers are saying, what the ballads emphasize, what verbs attach to your name.
- **How to reshape it before it sets.** The cow incident as a worked sequence:
    - accusation-frame hardens: "wizard turns child into cow"
    - fear-frame surfaces: "wizard is scary, village is afraid"
    - a true third frame displaces both: the sick-goat story
- **What to do if the accusation is true** — bridge to the companion "When The Accusation Is True" brief.
- **Why this isn't spin.** Spin manufactures facts; reframing surfaces true facts the first frame suppressed. Tie to the "no lying" contract language in `raw/gse-site/public-relations.md`.
