## Documented interactions

### Call 002 — [[delara-venn]] (incident follow-up)

Following an intrusion attempt at Delara's east wall, Corrin:

- Identified that the ward and detail response worked as designed but an outer-perimeter gap allowed the intruder to reach the wall unobserved.
- Proposed three escalation options: re-walk the east approach, add a second ring of lower-threshold wards at thirty paces, and upgrade from one-plus-on-call to a two-person standing watch (~70% cost increase on the guard line).
- Was asked about guard death benefits and indemnity and could not quote specific figures — flagged this as an operational gap.
