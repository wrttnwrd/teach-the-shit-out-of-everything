# Jenna Rill

**Summary**: Intake / Account Manager at [[green-slaad-enterprises]]. Handles prospect discovery calls and qualification for all service lines.

**Sources**: call-04-prospect-physical-iolanthe.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-17

## Role

Jenna manages initial prospect engagement and qualification at GSE. Documented responsibilities include:

- Conducting discovery calls to understand prospect needs and budget
- Qualifying prospects for appropriate service lines
- Booking assessments and coordinating travel logistics
- Managing the solo-practitioner tier waitlist

(source: call-04-prospect-physical-iolanthe.md)

## Documented interactions

### Call 004 — [[iolanthe-marsk]] (prospect discovery)

Iolanthe, a diviner living alone in a tower north of Waterdeep, called after reading GSE's "Divination Is Not A Security Strategy" post. Budget: ~20 gold/month, well below GSE's smallest retainer (60 gold/month).

Jenna:

- Was transparent about the budget gap rather than overselling.
- Recommended a one-time assessment (100 gold, two-day tower walk, written brief). Noted that many diviners in similar situations implement 90% of recommendations themselves.
- Mentioned the informal "lighter tier for solo practitioners" concept but was honest that it doesn't exist yet and has no timeline.
- Added Iolanthe to the waitlist (~14 people at time of call).
- Committed to flagging urgent findings during the assessment even if Iolanthe doesn't hire for remediation.

(source: call-04-prospect-physical-iolanthe.md)

## Notable qualities

Jenna demonstrates GSE's consultative sales approach: transparent about what can and cannot be delivered, willing to recommend self-service over forced engagement, and honest about internal product gaps rather than overselling vaporware. She also noted Iolanthe as a potential reference client due to her public following. (source: call-04-prospect-physical-iolanthe.md)
