# Sera Oak

**Summary**: Information Security Lead at [[green-slaad-enterprises]]. Handles counter-scrying sweeps, communication audits, and insider-threat investigations for the [[information-security]] practice.

## Role

Sera leads the information security practice at GSE. Documented responsibilities include:

- Scheduling and overseeing quarterly counter-scrying sweeps
- Diagnosing communication compromises (spoofed sendings, leaked signals)
- Conducting insider-threat investigations using decoy-audit methods
- Managing junior infosec team members (e.g., Veda)

(source: call-03-customer-infosec-halric.md)

## Documented interactions

### Call 003 — [[halric-bren]] (quarterly sweep scheduling + two issues)

**Issue 1 — Spoofed sending**: Halric received a sending that convincingly mimicked his cousin's private joke. Sera diagnosed the likely source as a leak from the cousin's side (direct or indirect), recommended a cousin-side audit (half-day engagement), and advised establishing a new shared signal created in person.

**Issue 2 — Suspected insider (apprentice)**: Halric's apprentice of two years appeared to have been reading his preparation notes. Sera was transparent about GSE's limits: the diagnostic (decoy monitoring) is within capability, but advising on what to do once confirmed is harder. Acknowledged ~6 prior cases with mixed outcomes and no written playbook. Offered to loop in [[mirella-thane]] for the relationship-management side, but respected Halric's preference to limit the circle.

Sera added a decoy-audit day to the Frostmoon 23 sweep and briefed team member Veda on the expanded scope.

(source: call-03-customer-infosec-halric.md)
