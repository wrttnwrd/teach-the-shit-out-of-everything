# Green Slaad Enterprises

**Summary**: A security and public relations firm serving wizards and arcane practitioners. Three service lines: physical security, information security, and public relations.

**Sources**: gse-site/index.md, gse-site/physical-security.md, gse-site/information-security.md, gse-site/public-relations.md, gse-calls/README.md

**Last updated**: 2026-04-17

## Overview

Green Slaad Enterprises (GSE) is a firm that provides security and reputation management services to wizards and other arcane practitioners. The name references the slaad — a creature of pure chaos — and the color green, which staff wear when blending in near client topiaries. The firm describes the joke as "order and chaos work together here." (source: gse-site/index.md)

The firm operates primarily along the Sword Coast, with engagements documented as far as Neverwinter and Baldur's Gate. There is no formal policy for work outside this region. (source: call-01-customer-pr-archibald.md)

## Services

GSE offers three integrated service lines:

### [[physical-security]]

Bodyguards from retired adventuring parties, ward calibration, sanctum hardening, travel escorts, and rapid response. Average response time: eleven minutes. Standard engagement begins with a two-day on-site threat assessment. (source: gse-site/physical-security.md)

### [[information-security]]

Counter-scrying sweeps, warded communications, scam triage, spellbook protection (including decoy "tarpit" books), and apprentice training. The team is staffed by ex-thieves who left guild politics behind. Sweeps find approximately one active [[scrying]] effect per wizard per month. (source: gse-site/information-security.md)

### [[public-relations]]

Reputation audits via bard networks, narrative development, good-works programs, crisis response, and bard retainers. Led by [[mirella-thane]]. The firm has handled twenty-three cow incidents and maintains a cow-incident playbook. (source: gse-site/public-relations.md)

## Core philosophy

The firm's central insight is that **the mob is the real threat**. Analysis of tower losses over the past century shows 92 out of 100 were caused by mobs of commoners, not rival spellcasters, extraplanar entities, or dragons. Yet wizards typically spend 90% of their defense budget on threats that account for 10% of actual losses. (source: gse-site/post-torches.md)

GSE positions itself against this pattern: practical security over flattering threat models, honest assessments over upsells, and a willingness to refer clients elsewhere when the firm cannot serve them well. (source: gse-site/index.md)

## Staff

The firm's staffing draws from three backgrounds:

- **Bodyguards**: Retired adventuring parties (source: gse-site/index.md)
- **Infosec team**: Ex-thieves who left the thieves' guild (source: gse-site/index.md)
- **PR staff**: Bards who shifted from tavern work to reputation management (source: gse-site/index.md)

## Intake process

First consultation is free. Contact via sending stone, courier, or enchanted raven. The firm collects ward-calibration data at the start of any retainer — a protocol added after the [[the-cow-incident]] response was slowed by client wards. (source: gse-site/index.md, gse-site/post-cow.md)
